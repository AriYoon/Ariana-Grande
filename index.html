First album : Yours Truly

This is the first full-length album released on August 30, 2013. She hated the same bubblegum pop genre as her previous debut single. So she released this album in order to show her music in the style of R&B in 1990.


Song : Honeymoon Avenue, Baby I, Right There, Tattooed Heart, Lovin' It, Piano, Daydreamin', The Way

Second album : My Everything

This album was released on August 22, 2014, which was a huge hit both at home and abroad. The genres of this album are R&B, Pop, and even Dance-Pop.


Song : Intro, Problem, One Last Time, Why Try, Break Free, Best Mistake, Be My Baby, Break Your Heart Right Back, Bang Bang, Love me harder....

Third album : Dangerous Woman

On May 20, the third album of pop fairy Ariana Grande was released. This is a mature album that shows more distinctive colors and genres of Grande. The third album is more direct than the previous one.


Song : Mooonlight, Dangerous Woman, Side to Side, Let Me Love You, Greedy, I Don't Care, Touch It...

Fourth album : Sweetener

Ariana Grande will release her fourth album on August 17, 2018. Unlike the previous album, there were many very sweet and lovely songs.


Song : raindrops, blazed, God is a woman, sweetener, everytime, No Tears Left to Cry, better off, goodnight n go

Fifth album : thank u, next

On February 8, Ariana Grande's fifth album was released. This album is made up of only Ariana Grande's vocals, with no other artist's featuring.


Song : imagine, needy, NASA, 7 rings, thank u, next, make up...
